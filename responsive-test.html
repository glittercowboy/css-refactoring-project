9. Media Query Mixin Examples

The following examples demonstrate how our media query mixins work in practice. The colored boxes below will change at different breakpoints:

sm-up Example: This box is yellow by default, but will turn green at the small breakpoint (480px) and above.

md-up Example: This box is yellow by default, but will turn blue at the medium breakpoint (768px) and above.

lg-up Example: This box is yellow by default, but will turn purple at the large breakpoint (1024px) and above.

xs-only Example: This box is yellow by default, but will turn red only on extra small screens (below 480px).
